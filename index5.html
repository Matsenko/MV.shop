Shipment and payment

Buyers of Cherkasy and the region can order and purchase the necessary goods at the address: Cherkasy, st. Khreschatyk, 188 (mn. "MV_shop").

Nonresident buyers can pre-order the necessary assortment by e-mail MV_shop@gmail.com (for private individuals) or MV_shop.buh@gmail.com (for FOP, DP, TOV and others).

For sending questions, call by phone

  • private customers: 0472-38-64-21, 067-798-47-47, 050-81-38-222
  • enterprises and FOP: 0472-56-37-65, 0472-33-07-68

When placing an order, make a list of the necessary goods indicating

  • full name, type of case and manufacturer, if necessary, quantity.

The following data must also be indicated:

  • surname, name, patronymic of the recipient of the goods, or the full name of the organization;
  • place of receipt (settlement);
  • preferred carrier and its warehouse;
  • contact telephone number (in long-distance format), or mobile phone number for direct communication with the customer.

After clarifying the availability of the ordered items, a manager will contact you additionally to coordinate delivery options, and will also issue an invoice for payment. The cost of packaging and delivery of the order to the courier service is 10 UAH. for orders up to 300 UAH., above - for free. Payment can be made:

  • cash on delivery through "New post";
  • through replenishment of the Privatbank card of the seller;
  • through the seller’s bank account for enterprises and organizations, VAT payers.

Shipment of goods is carried out after payment, as well as receipt of the necessary copies of documents for the issuance of expenditure and tax invoices and in cases of contractual relations. By prior arrangement, we can deliver the goods you purchased to the address indicated in the application:

  • By courier to the train, or intercity bus.
  • parcel in Ukraine by the carrier company “New Mail” (to cities and towns where there are representative offices) - according to the current tariffs of companies.
