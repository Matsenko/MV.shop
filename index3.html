About company

The MV_shop in its assortment has a wide selection of imported and domestic electronic components, components, devices and tools. From the warehouse of the store, retail and wholesale supply of parts for repair, after-sales service and industrial production is carried out. We also deliver components on order, from two days to three months. Delivery of goods is carried out on the territory of Ukraine through carriers convenient for the buyer.

The range of products supplied includes:

  • active components (microcircuits, transistors, diodes, thyristors, sensors, power modules)
  • passive components (resistors, capacitors, fuses, inductance, filters, transformers, including TDKS);
  • cases, cassettes for electronic components;
  • batteries (rechargeable batteries, batteries).
