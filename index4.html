How to do order

Dear buyer, on the site we have created for you a very convenient service, using which you can easily place an order. There are two ways to find the product you are interested in. With the help of a catalog, where all the goods are sorted by categories, namely: electronic components, components and materials for electronics, instruments and tools - with the following varieties of goods. Or you can enter a name in the search box and the system will find exactly what you need if this product is on sale. All prices listed on the website are relevant. There is a discount for regular buyers and a system has been developed for entrepreneurs and wholesalers

The selection of products is carried out under each unit with the button "Buy" and automatically transferred to the basket. At the end of the list formation, register (for new clients) or pass authorization (for regular customers). Your personal account is another advantage on the site, with the help of it you will be able to communicate and view the history of your own orders.
